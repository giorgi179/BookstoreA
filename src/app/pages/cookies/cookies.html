Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how the site is used so we can improve it.

Types of Cookies We Use

Category Purpose Duration
Necessary Authentication, session management, security Session / 30 days
Preferences Remembering display settings and selections 1 year
Analytics Understanding how visitors use the site 1 year
Marketing Personalized recommendations and newsletters 1 year

Manage Your Preferences

You can choose which categories of optional cookies to allow below. Necessary cookies cannot be disabled, as they are required for the site to function.

Necessary

Required for login, security, and core site functionality. Always active.

Preferences

Remembers choices like your selected tab or display settings.

Analytics

Helps us understand site usage so we can improve the experience.

Marketing

Used for personalized recommendations and newsletter content.

Managing Cookies in Your Browser

In addition to the controls above, most browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can delete existing cookies, block cookies from specific sites, or block third-party cookies entirely. Note that disabling necessary cookies may affect your ability to sign in or use certain features.
